Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Overview

Volocal is a fully on-device voice AI assistant. Your privacy is fundamental to how this app is built — all speech recognition, language model inference, and text-to-speech processing happen entirely on your device.

Data Collection

Volocal does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

  • Your voice input is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to any server.
  • Conversations are not recorded, logged, or stored beyond the current session.
  • No analytics, tracking, or telemetry data is collected.
  • No account or sign-in is required.

Microphone Access

Volocal requests microphone access solely to capture your voice for on-device speech-to-text processing. Audio data is processed in real-time and is not stored or transmitted.

Network Usage

Volocal requires an internet connection only during the initial setup to download the AI models (~2.3 GB). After the models are downloaded, the app functions entirely offline. No data is sent from the app to any server at any time.

Third-Party Services

Volocal does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Volocal does not collect any data from any users, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at github.com/fikrikarim/volocal.
